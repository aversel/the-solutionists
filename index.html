"Annual audits and sampling-based testing cannot protect organizations moving at the speed of AI and digital transformation."

The Solutionists was built by senior practitioners who spent decades inside the world's largest audit and risk networks — and grew frustrated by the gap between what Fortune 500 boards needed and what legacy models could deliver. We set out to close that gap permanently.

We operate as an elite, specialist advisory firm with no large delivery pyramids and no off-shored junior teams. Every engagement is resourced with senior professionals who combine deep technical expertise with the strategic acumen to communicate directly at board level. We call it the High-Impact Specialist Pod model — small, accountable, senior, and extraordinarily effective.

Integrity. These aren't compliance checkboxes for us. They're the architecture of every engagement we conduct.
